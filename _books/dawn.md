`Dawn` is the fiction side of Chad O. Grant's public catalog. The premise is
not built around small personal drama. It is built around systems, conflict,
and the return of forces that can reorder an already unstable world.

That matters because the broader Chad O. Grant body of work keeps circling the
same question in different forms: what happens when pressure builds inside a
system that looks stable on the surface. In the macro work that shows up in
markets, states, and institutions. In `Dawn`, it shows up in a speculative
universe where old structures are no longer enough to contain what is coming.
